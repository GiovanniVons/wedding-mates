Why Choose A Celebrant, When You Could Choose A Mate?
Finding the right celebrant is tough
You scroll through endless websites, check availability, and hope a stranger you’ve never met will nail the most special day of your life. But why leave it to chance? Why choose a stranger when you can choose a friend?
Now picture a more personal option ...
You’re standing in front of everyone you love, the music fades, your best mate steps forward. Not with a generic script, but with a cheeky grin. With stories, laughter, and a warmth only someone you truly love can bring.
They open with a joke, and just as the laughter settles, they hit you with words full of heart and sentiment before inviting you to share a kiss, then sparking a wave of cheers and applause in celebration of your official union.
Wedding Mates create ceremonies written to be felt
We provide your mate with wedding ceremony training and you with all the legal requirements. The result is deeply intimate, heartfelt ceremony full of laughter and magic. An experience you and your guests will cherish forever.
