## Business Overview

**Wedding Mates** (brand also appears as **Let's Get Wed**, email domain `letsgetwed.com.au`) is an Australian wedding business with one ownable idea: **instead of hiring a stranger celebrant, the couple's own best mate leads the ceremony** ("GET WED YOUR WAY", "Say 'I do' in the most meaningful way possible, with your best mate marrying you"). The business removes the two blockers to a friend-led ceremony: (1) the friend does not know how to write or deliver a ceremony, and (2) the legal requirements. Wedding Mates solves both.

**What the client sells (one $950 AUD package):**
- **The Wedding Ceremony Blueprint** -- an 8-module gated online course (video overview + accompanying long-form text + downloadable templates per module) that trains the chosen "mate" to interview the couple, write the love story, structure the ceremony, add music/readings/vows, cue the other participants, and deliver with confidence.
- **The legal layer** -- a registered celebrant handles all the legal paperwork and pre-ceremony requirements before the day, so the friend can focus on delivering the experience. ("We Handle the Legals.")
- **Onboarding video call** for the couple and their chosen celebrant.
- **Templates, readings library, performance strategies, sample scripts.**

**Paid optional extras** (booking add-ons): Official Marriage Certificate $69, Registered Celebrant in Attendance on the day $299, Zoom Rehearsal with celebrant $99, Custom Script Review $129.

**Founder:** Sarah. Origin story: became a registered celebrant (300+ hours of study) to marry her best friend in 2019, found it the most personal and rewarding way to officiate, and turned the coaching she gave friends into the Blueprint so more people can do the same.

**Two-sided product (important for IA + copy):** there are two distinct users.
- **The Couple** = the buyer and primary conversion target. They pay the $950, choose their mate, and want an intimate, personal, stress-free, legally-sorted ceremony.
- **The Mate** = the chosen friend/sibling/parent who becomes the "unofficial celebrant" and is the actual student of the gated course. They need confidence, structure, and reassurance.
The marketing site sells the couple; the gated course serves the mate. The booking flow sits between them (couple buys, both get access + the onboarding call).

**Legal accuracy note (flag for Phase 1 + legal review):** In Australia, only a Commonwealth-registered marriage celebrant can legally solemnise a marriage. Wedding Mates's model is that a **registered celebrant handles the legal solemnisation/paperwork** while the friend delivers the ceremony experience. Marketing language ("your mate can really marry you") is an emotional framing of a hybrid arrangement, not a claim that an untrained friend signs the legal marriage. Copy must keep the warmth while staying truthful about who does the legal part. **Client to confirm the exact legal mechanism** (does the registered celebrant attend and legally officiate while the friend "leads", or is the legal marriage registered separately/after?). The booking doc's < 4-week note ("your legal marriage will need to be registered after the ceremony") suggests a separate legal-registration step.

## Target Audiences

**Primary -- The Couple (buyer).**
- Engaged couples in Australia, broadly 25-40, planning a wedding 3+ months out.
- Want a ceremony that feels personal, warm, funny, and authentic rather than a generic script delivered by a stranger.
- Value intimacy and story over formality; likely also budget-aware ("less than the cost of a good celebrant").
- Mobile-first researchers; emotionally driven decision, reassurance-seeking on the legal/"is this allowed?" question.
- Conversion trigger: realising a friend leading the ceremony is both possible and legally sorted.

**Secondary -- The Mate (course user / unofficial celebrant).**
- A close friend, sibling, or parent chosen by the couple. Often nervous, never done public speaking or ceremony-writing.
- Needs: a clear step-by-step path, templates, confidence/nerves support, and the reassurance that "the legal side is fully handled by us."
- Engages deeply with the gated course over several weeks (the doc estimates ~15-17 hours of work across course + interview + writing + rehearsal).

**Tertiary -- Gift-givers / wedding-party influencers** who might suggest or gift the service.

## Competitive Landscape

- **Traditional registered marriage celebrants** (the default). Typically $600-$1,200+ in AU. Strength: legally complete, experienced. Weakness: a stranger delivering your most personal moment. Wedding Mates's wedge: the person who knows you best leads it, for comparable or lower cost.
- **DIY / online ceremony-script generators & celebrant directories** (e.g. script templates, "write your own vows" sites, celebrant marketplaces). Strength: cheap/flexible. Weakness: no legal coverage, no coaching, no delivery confidence. Wedding Mates bundles training + legals.
- **Generic online-course platforms** (Teachable/Kajabi wedding or public-speaking courses). Strength: structured learning. Weakness: not wedding-legal-specific, no celebrant service. Wedding Mates is purpose-built and ends in a legally valid ceremony.
- **Self-uniting / friend-officiant services** (more common in the US; in AU constrained by the registered-celebrant requirement). Wedding Mates's AU-specific legal handling is the differentiator.

**Ownable position:** the only offer that trains *your own person* to deliver a professional-standard ceremony AND quietly handles the Australian legal requirements, so a friend-led wedding is both magical and valid.

## Design Constraints

- **Three surfaces, one brand:** (A) public marketing site, (B) 7-step booking + payment wizard, (C) login-gated members course. The design system must cover all three (marketing sections, multi-step form UI, long-form lesson reading UI, auth screens) plus a `/design-book`.
- **Two audiences:** marketing speaks to the couple; the course speaks to the mate. The voice shifts inside the gated area.
- **Functional product:** real Supabase auth, live Stripe payments (AUD), Resend transactional email, RLS-gated course content. Server runtime required (not a static export).
- **Australian context:** AUD pricing, AU English, AU legal framing (registered celebrant handles legals), AU Privacy Act for the PII collected at booking (both partners' names, mobile, suburb, celebrant contacts).
- **Mobile-first:** wedding research and course reading are mobile-heavy; the booking wizard and lesson reading must be excellent on phones.
- **Accessibility:** WCAG AA (course is long-form reading; the booking form must be fully labelled; pre-`prefers-reduced-motion`).
- **Consent/analytics:** integrate Vonzie Nexus CMP post-launch; analytics gated behind consent; no real events on localhost.
- **Content honesty:** never fabricate testimonials, blog posts, FAQ answers, or legal terms (all are gaps on the live site). Keep the legal claims truthful.
- **Course media:** all 8 module videos + the intro video are unavailable; build placeholder video slots with a single swap-point.

## Website Goals

1. **Primary conversion -- complete the $950 Wedding Mates booking** (the 7-step portal, ending in payment + course access). Every marketing page funnels here. One unmistakable primary action: "Start Your Journey" / "Book Now".
2. **Secondary -- course access & engagement for the mate** (sign in, progress through the 8 modules, download templates). Retention/clarity goal, not a sale.
3. **Trust-building** -- answer the two anxieties (legality + can-my-friend-do-it), carry the founder story, and make the legal handling credible. (Real reviews/testimonials to come from the client.)
4. **Capture qualified interest** for couples not ready to buy (consult/contact, FAQ).
